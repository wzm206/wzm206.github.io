# ✨ About Me
<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>


I am Zengmao Wang (王增茂), a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), where I have been studying since 2023. I am supervised by Prof. [Wei Gao](https://weigao-3dv.github.io/) and Prof. [Shuhan Shen](http://www.3dv.ac.cn/faculty/shs/). Prior to that, I received my Bachelor’s degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.

My research interests lie in multimodal understanding and generation. In particular, I am interested in developing intelligent agents that can actively explore, adapt, and evolve in real-world environments.
